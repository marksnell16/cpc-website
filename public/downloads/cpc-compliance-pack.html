Modern Slavery Act Compliance
Modern Slavery Statement — Modern Slavery Act 2018 (Cth)
CPC publishes an annual Modern Slavery Statement in compliance with the Modern Slavery Act 2018 (Cth). The statement is registered on the Australian Border Force Modern Slavery Register and is available for download at cpclean.com.au/due-diligence.

Statement covers: Identification of modern slavery risks in CPC's operations and supply chains · Due diligence processes applied · Actions taken to address identified risks · Effectiveness assessment · Key approval and sign-off by CPC's responsible entity.

CPC's supply chain management includes direct employment preference to reduce subcontracting risk, supplier screening for labour compliance, Fair Work Act compliance across all direct operations, and documented grievance mechanisms for all workforce.
