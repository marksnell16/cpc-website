H2
Who We Are
CPC is a national commercial cleaning company with permanent operations across QLD, NSW, NT, WA and SA. We are not a franchise, a labour hire business or a broker. We directly employ our cleaning workforce, manage our own supervision structure and hold all required certifications under our own organisational name. This matters because accountability cannot be subcontracted.
H2
What Makes Us Different
Most commercial cleaning companies serve any client in any environment. CPC focuses deliberately on regulated, complex and accountability-driven environments — government, councils, industrial, education and commercial. This focus means our management systems, training programs and compliance frameworks are purpose-built for these environments, not retrofitted to them.
H2
Our Certifications
ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management System
ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management System
ISO 45001:2018 — WHS Management System

All three certifications are held from JAS-ANZ accredited certification bodies and are subject to annual surveillance audits. Certificates are available for download in the Due Diligence Hub.
H2
Carbon Neutral Program
CPC operates a verified carbon neutral cleaning program — measuring Scope 1, 2 and 3 emissions annually, implementing reduction initiatives (fleet transition, GreenPower, eco-product programs) and retiring verified offsets through Gold Standard and ACCU-registered projects. Client-level carbon reporting is available through the CPC client portal.
H2
National Operations
Permanent regional teams are based in QLD, NSW, NT, WA and SA. Each region has dedicated area managers, supervisors and support staff. National governance is provided through a central management structure. We do not use fly-in-fly-out management or subcontract regional delivery to third parties. Consistent standards are maintained through shared systems, not geography.
H2
Workforce and Ethics
CPC directly employs the majority of its cleaning workforce. We publish an annual Modern Slavery Statement aligned to the Commonwealth Modern Slavery Act 2018. Our workforce development programs include structured induction, site-specific training and career pathway support. We do not compete on labour cost reduction — we compete on capability and compliance.
H2
Technology and Reporting
Digital audit platforms are used across all CPC contracts — providing real-time inspection data, photo evidence of defects, timestamped completion records and automated performance reporting. The CPC client portal gives contract clients direct access to their audit scores, carbon data, invoices, compliance documents and an AI-powered performance assistant.
H2
Tender and Procurement Support
CPC supports formal procurement processes with complete credential packs, reference site arrangements and the Due Diligence Hub — a structured online resource with all certifications, insurance documents, WHS policies, modern slavery statements and case studies in one place. We have experience responding to Commonwealth, state and local government tender requirements.
H2
Talk to CPC
If your organisation operates in a regulated or complex cleaning environment, we welcome enquiries about our capability, our credentials and our approach. Contact us to speak with a sector specialist, request a proposal or access the Due Diligence Hub for procurement evaluation.

📋 Implementation Note
The Client Portal is built as a complete standalone HTML file (cpc-client-portal.html). Download from the artifact panel, rename from .txt to .html, and open in any browser. For production deployment: host on Vercel or Netlify for a stable URL. Issue client login credentials and share the portal URL. The AI Assistant requires the Anthropic API key to be configured server-side for production use.
